Rules of Engagement

Objective

Guess the hidden locations of your enemy's ships; sink all their ships before they sink yours. The goal of the game is to sink all of the opponent's ships by correctly guessing their location on the grid.

Ships

Each side has five ships, and each ship can take a certain number of hits.

The five ships are:

  • Carrier - Five Hits
  • Battleship - Four Hits
  • Cruiser - Three Hits
  • Submarine - Three Hits
  • Destroyer - Two Hits

Gameplay

The battlefield consists of two grids. One grid is used to hide your ships from your enemy. The other grid is used to record the shots fired toward your opponent, and to document whether those shots were hits or misses.

Before the battle begins, each opponent secretly positions their own five ships on their own grid. Each ship must be placed horizontally or vertically — not diagonally — across grid spaces, and the ships can't hang off the grid. Ships can touch each other, but they can't occupy the same grid space. Ship positions cannot be changed once the battle has commenced.
